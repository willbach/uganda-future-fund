Our Portfolio

Gorilla Summit Coffee

Gorilla Summit Coffee selects only the highest quality beans from thousands of cooperative growers across Uganda. Resulting from extensive farmer training and outreach, Gorilla Summit Coffee has received high marks from international coffee cuppings in Europe and the United States.

Uganda Avocados

Uganda Avocados is developing the value chain for the export of processed and fresh avocados from Uganda’s most fertile regions to address skyrocketing global demand and constrained global supply.

About

The Uganda Future Fund (TUFF) deploys capital and human resources in high growth Uganda-based businesses delivering financial, environmental, and social returns. TUFF invests in food, agriculture, clean technology, energy, and healthcare ventures poised to be both domestic market leaders and global export players.

TUFF partners with Uganda’s most capable business leaders, managers, and operators and garners support from stakeholders from the highest levels of commerce and government. Linking Ugandan businesses with international markets and networks, TUFF maximizes both financial returns and social impact to communities across East Africa.

In addition to capital, TUFF provides strategic, operational, and technical guidance to its portfolio companies to achieve competitive advantage. TUFF’s robust network of United States-based scientists, researchers and investors delivers world class expertise to Uganda’s most promising changemakers.
